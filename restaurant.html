Dine At’
Deluxe Dining Restaurant

Food with twist offering modern and local dining experience on a platter served with a smile. The thing about Deluxe dining is that it refuses to be shut inside a jar of ingredients. Dishes here take an inspiration from all over the world allowing our professional chefs to play with cuisines, modernize traditional recipes and experiment with ingredients. Although fun and lively, yet deeply rooted in tradition that span the length and breadth of this country.

We care what you consume by harvesting our own herbs, seasonal vegetables and fruits in our own farm located at the outskirts of the city. Our team likes to change things up, but what doesn’t is the quality of what’s on your plate and the warmth with which it is presented to you.

Hours

  • Breakfast: 7 am to 10:30 am.

  • Lunch from 12 noon to 3 pm.

  • Dinner from 7 pm to 11 pm.

  • Last order 10:30 pm.

'Alfresco Area’

Our Restaurant has an alfresco area if guests would like to sit outside and enjoy the beautiful weather the capital has to offer. The alfresco area is also a designated smoking area.

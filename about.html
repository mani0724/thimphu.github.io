About Us

Thimphu Deluxe
Your Home Away From Home

Located in the northwestern part of Thimphu city, Thimphu Deluxe offers the best view of the capital. Our property boasts a relaxed ambience of art and tradition infused to bring you the comfort of home but also with the amenities of a modern property. We are located in a place where our guests can leisurely walk to some of the historic monuments the city has to offer; from one of the largest seated statues of Gautama Buddha to the National Memorial Stupa dedicated to the 3 rd Druk Gyalpo His Majesty Jigme Dorji Wangchuck.

Stay with us and experience the taste of local and international cuisines, prepared by the hands of our experts who love showcasing their talents. Whether it is a wedding ceremony in a traditional Bhutanese style or a conference, Thimphu Deluxe has the best space to offer.

The Thimphu Deluxe Hotel
Your Home Away From Home

On your journey to experience the culture and traditions of the Land of the Thunder Dragon, we invite you to stay with us at the Thimphu Deluxe Hotel. Boasting a relaxing ambience filled with art and vibrant architecture, at the Thimphu Deluxe Hotel you will discover the tradition and comfort of contemporary hospitality. We provide you with both local as well as international standards of cuisine. Our services make it possible to host any event, from a traditional wedding ceremony to modern business conferences.

Located in the north-western part of Thimphu city, our property offers the best view of the capital. Our guests can leisurely walk to some of the historic monuments the city has to offer; from one of the largest seated statues of Gautama Buddha to the National Memorial Stupa dedicated to the 3rd Druk Gyalpo His Majesty Jigme Dorji Wangchuck.

We assure you that your stay will be a memorable one and ensure that the luxuries of your home will not be lacking in ours.
